# 5.2.2 Manufactured SP Life Cycle States

The LockingSP’s OFS could be either the Manufactured-Inactive or the Manufactured state. The Manufactured-toManufactured optional state transition may occur when the LockingSP’s OFS is the Manufactured state. In an implementation where the LockingSP’s OFS is the Manufactured state, then the Revert method would revert the LockingSP from Manufactured back to Manufactured while still reverting all LockingSP tables back to the OFS.
