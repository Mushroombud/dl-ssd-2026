# 4.3.5.4 MBR (M)

- 4.3.5.4 MBR (M) The MBR minimum size SHALL be 128 MB (0x08000000). The initial contents of the MBR table SHALL be vendor unique.
- 4.3.5.5 K_AES_128 or K_AES_256 (M) At least one of the following tables (Table 47 and Table 48) SHALL be supported.
