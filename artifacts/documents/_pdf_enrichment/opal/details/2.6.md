# 2.6 Table Management

- 2.6 Table Management This specification defines the mandatory tables and mandatory/optional table rows delivered by the Storage Device manufacturer. The creation or deletion of tables after manufacturing is outside the scope of this specification. The creation or deletion of table rows post-manufacturing is outside the scope of this specification.
